Respond to Crisis with Mutual Aid

Why Take Direct Action

Government Intervention intends to preserve societal structure 1 instead of protecting people, "dictating constraints to employers". 2

With COVID-19, lots of people in the service industry are impacted. Loss of income with cascading effects (e.g. rent, leaving school, long term health).

This site offers one way to help. Donate that money you would have spent on coffee (or tea or pastries or w/e) anyway to the workers at the shops you frequent.

We acknowledge this is just one part of a much larger context. In particular:

  • We should be pushing policymakers to provide policies that create social security blankets.
  • There are a lot of sectors that are hurting in light of the pandemic that arenâ€™t covered here.
  • Mutual aid and community in general is important (not just in light of coronavirus). So far, social welfare and safety nets have only contributed to keeping alive the ideas of capitalism. If we are to challenge those ideas, we must take matters into our own hands. We are the ones who we've been waiting for!
